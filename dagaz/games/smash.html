Smash Remix

by The_Smashfather

Description

Immerse yourself in a world of mind-blowing fights in Smash Remix and get ready to test your fighting skills! Enjoy this fun mod inspired by the classic Super Smash Bros. as you live a thrilling experience surrounded by the best and bravest characters from classic video games.


Controls

    Arrow Keys: Control movement

    Enter: Start/Pause

    Hover and click the gamepad icon to view/change controls.
